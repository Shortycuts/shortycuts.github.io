About Shortycuts

First, welcome to the website of Shortycuts.

I'm a German shortcuts developer with vietnamese roots. Sure, my real name is not Shortycuts, but you can address me that way. I started creating shortcuts when the "Shortcuts" app was known as "Workflow", so I have some experiences in this section. In addition to developing and tweaking shortcuts, I have some hobbies, like play the piano or meet friends and enjoy with them the wonderful life with fun activities, sports and much more. Cheers, Shortycuts

Values of Shortycuts

Quality is the most important thing in Shortycuts's shortcut series. I focus on fixing bugs and making small improvements, instead of publishing big updates with new features, even though the shortcut is still buggy. If everything seems to be stable, big updates with new functions will follow. It's like a school lesson - quality before quantity.
